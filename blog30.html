3 Harsh Money Truths I Wish I Learned Earlier

Posted on July 23, 2025

Nobody teaches you the raw truth about money in school. It’s mostly sugarcoated or ignored. But money is a game — and if you don’t learn the rules early, you pay for it later.

💸 1. Time is more expensive than money

You can always make more money. But once time is gone, it never comes back. Stop wasting years undercharging, overthinking, or waiting to “feel ready.”

📉 2. Saving alone won’t make you rich

Cutting back on coffee won’t change your life. Learning to create value, sell it, and scale it will. Saving is defensive. Wealth comes from playing offense.

🔒 3. No one’s coming to save you

Don’t rely on “luck” or someone discovering you. Start the thing. Sell the skill. Build the system. Take responsibility for the income you want.

Once you stop expecting money to find you — and start treating it like a craft to master — everything changes.

“Money follows momentum. Not intentions.”
